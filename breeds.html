Cat Breeds

Discover the unique characteristics of different cat breeds

Siamese

Vocal Social Intelligent

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat. Known for their striking blue eyes and color-point coat, they are extremely social and vocal cats that form strong bonds with their owners.

Weight: 8-15 lbs | Lifespan: 15-20 years

Maine Coon

Gentle Large Friendly

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. With its distinctive physical appearance and valuable hunting skills, it's one of the oldest natural breeds in North America.

Weight: 10-25 lbs | Lifespan: 12-15 years

Scottish Fold

Sweet Adaptable Unique ears

Scottish Folds are known for their distinctive folded ears that give them an owl-like appearance. They have a sweet, gentle temperament and adapt well to various living situations.

Weight: 6-13 lbs | Lifespan: 11-14 years
