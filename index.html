ABOUT CATS

Cats have a long history of domestication, dating back to ancient civilizations. Early documentation shows they were valued for their ability to hunt pests and their elegant companionship. Over time, cats have become beloved pets worldwide.

CAT BREEDS

Diverse varieties of cats exist today, with distinct breeds featuring unique characteristics, appearances, and personalities. From playful tabbies to majestic Maine Coons, there's a breed for every cat lover.
