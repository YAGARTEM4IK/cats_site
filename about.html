About DarkCats

Discover the story behind our passion for feline beauty

Our Mission

At DarkCats, we're dedicated to showcasing the mysterious and elegant side of our feline friends. We believe cats are more than just pets - they're works of art, companions with deep personalities, and creatures of incredible beauty.

Our mission is to create a space where cat lovers can appreciate the darker, more dramatic aesthetic of these amazing animals, while learning about their care, behavior, and unique characteristics.

Our Story

DarkCats began in 2018 as a personal photography project by founder Alex Whiskers. What started as a way to capture the beauty of his black cat, Shadow, quickly grew into a community of photographers and cat enthusiasts.

Today, we're a team of 10 passionate individuals working across three countries, united by our love for cats and dark aesthetics. We collaborate with shelters, photographers, and cat owners worldwide to bring you the most stunning feline content.
